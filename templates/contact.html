FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MediSense free to use?

Yes! Our symptom analysis and disease prediction features are completely free. You only pay when you book an appointment with a doctor.

How accurate are the AI predictions?

Our AI model is trained on extensive medical data and continuously improved. However, it's designed as an assistive tool - always consult a professional for final diagnosis.

Is my medical data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and follow strict data privacy protocols. Your health information is never shared with third parties.

Can I book appointments through the platform?

Yes! After getting your AI diagnosis, you can directly book appointments with relevant specialists through our integrated booking system.
